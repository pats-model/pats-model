Parcel Ascent Tracing System (PATS) is the numerical model for convective parcel ascent simulation in three-dimension developed as a Master’s project at the School of Environmental Sciences at the University of East Anglia.

The model predicts how convective air parcel behaves in conditions much more realistic then those assumed by classical parcel theory.

The model is currently under development.
